Rider Tracker Pro is a Progressive Web App (PWA) specifically designed for delivery riders who want to maintain precise control over their actual earnings and vehicle operating costs.

Unlike standard trackers, this app doesn't just calculate gross income; it automatically deducts taxes (withholding tax) and real-time fuel costs, providing a Real Net Profit calculation based on vehicle efficiency.

ğŸš€ Key Features:
Target Tracking: Set a monthly net goal and follow your progress with a dynamic percentage bar.

Refueling Log: Record every gas station visit with price per liter, total spend, and total liters added.

Total Privacy: No external databases. All data is saved exclusively on your device using Local Storage.
